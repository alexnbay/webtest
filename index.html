ALONG THE PATH
TO FORGIVENESS

This site accompanies the book by the same title, published by Carr C. Krueger, II in 2022. The book contains stories of some truly extraordinary human beings. It is dedicated to exploring their exceptional lives, to realistically evaluating their failings, and to exploring their triumph to overcome and forgive.

This site gives access to additional content including maps and photos. It also contains a section where you can provide your feedback on the book or this site. I enthusiastically welcome your comments. I hope you gain knowledge and peace as you review this material.
